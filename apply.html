Apply for MCG

If you meet all of the requirements, you can apply for ETS2MCG member.

Requirements

  1. You are at least 14 years old.
  2. You have to play ETS2 for at least 100 hours.
  3. Regularly join us in convoys/events.
  4. We currently support languages: English, Thailand, Vietnam, Korea, Bahasa Indonesia, Bahasa Malaysia, Taiwan.
  5. Make sure you don't have more than 3 bans on TruckersMP's profile.

Benefits

Joining ETS2MCG team, you will have a plenty amount of benefits.

  • You can apply for a DLC gift event.
  • You can quickly check and participate in events within and outside our team.
  • You can play games with your team members and build friendships.
  • You can experience our team's unique fast driving!

How to apply

If you want to join the team, you can join the Discord server and ask your server administrator to join. Click the link below to join our Discord server.
